Crypto'em

Cryptography and coding plays an essential role in effective security and reliability for data communication , processing and storage. Crypto'em is curated and thought provoking event that consists of three rounds which is based on brain stimulating puzzles and codes.

ROUND 1:
-This round presents the participants with 20 quiz questions.
- Each of these questions are based on crptographic puzzles.
-And each participant is expected to solve maximum of these questions to get shortlisted for next round.

ROUND 2:
- It’s a Coding round wherein students will be given 3 Cipher texts to break and they will have to automate the process through code.
- Will be conducted on Hackerrank platform. Programs can be written using C C++ or Python.
- Grading will be based on the number of test cases passed and the time taken to break each cipher.

ROUND 3:
-The shortlisted participants should find a solution for the given real world problem. Programs can be written in C, C++ or Python and can be compiled in any online compiler.
- They will be given 1 hr time and will be asked to switch on their camera and will be monitored.

Mind Drill

If you are one among those who work tirelessly with circuits, this is for you. Mind drill tests the very basics of electronics! Showcase your skills at designing and implementing electronic circuits. All that you need to do is to brush up on your concepts and get ready to unleash your designing skills in the 'ELECTRONICS' field.

ROUND 1: Electronics Quiz
- It consists of multiple-choice questions to test your technical knowledge.
- 25 questions are to be solved in 20 minutes. Based on the marks obtained participants will be short listed for the next round.
- The test can be taken only once.

ROUND 2: Circuit Debugging Quiz
- It consists of multiple-choice questions to test your knowledge in circuit designing and to identify the error free circuit.
- 10 questions are to be solved in 15 mins. Based on the marks obtained, participants shall be short listed for the final round.

ROUND 3: Circuit Designing
- Participants are given with a set of components. From that they can choose required components for each question and build a circuit.
- It consists of 5 questions of different difficulty levels and duration is 35 minutes.
- Participants should draw the circuit in paper and upload it before the stimulated time.
- Mark allocation and Judging criteria will be sent before the start of this round.
